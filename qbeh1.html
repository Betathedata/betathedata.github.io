WHAT IS QBEH-1?

QBEH-1: The Crafters Cube is a puzzle platformer that takes you on a journey through a variety of worlds, each containing a multitude of challenges and secrets for you to uncover. Each level presents you with a set of obstacles, which you can overcome by collecting cubes and using them to create platforms, allowing you to reach the portal to the next level. There are a finite number of cubes in each level, and they can only be placed upon yellow tiles and other cubes, making each level an increasingly difficult puzzle that challenges you to anticipate consequences, and plan your moves in advance.
