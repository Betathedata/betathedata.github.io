What is Cookie Clicker?

Cookie Clicker is a Javascript game released by Orteil on August 10, 2013. It is an "incrementer" game, as proclaimed by Orteil. The point of the game is to bake cookies by clicking on a giant cookie until you have enough cookies to buy upgrades. The seemingly endless gameplay makes it a game that can last an indefinite amount of time, or at least until your device can no longer run it.

Minecraft Remake?

This website is where the Minecraft remake of the original game, Cookie Clicker, will be hosted. The game will not be released until 1.9 is fully released. This version is a direct remake of the original version of the game, it is done using Command blocks, a sort of "Scripting" mechanism for Minecraft.

Why are you making it?

I am making Cookie Clicker because I find it fun and interesting. Yes, I know that there are probably hundreds of Cookie Clicker maps already out there, but I havent seen any so far with infinite expansion levels, functional shops, functional clicking systems etc. So I thought I'd have a shot at it.
