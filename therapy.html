What is this page?

This is the page in which I will host a game that I am submitting for my Sixth form coursework, I am going to publically release the game so I can start a portfolio for University. It is a recreation of a Horror themed Adventure map originally made for a game called Minecraft.

What is it about?

The storyline is not final as it may change during the creation. The storyline is focused around a man called Jim, a patient at a small mental asylum, gets visited by his 14th therapist who is very interested in the traumatising experience that he had as a child. Experience the story as Jim tells it, exploring the house as his 6 year old self.

When will this be available?

This game has to be finished by the end of my coursework which is around May. Depending on the quality and the amount of effort that I put into this, I may attempt to place the game on Steam through Steam Greenlight.
